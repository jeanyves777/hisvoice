================================================================================
  SECTION 1 — VISION & PROJECT SCOPE
================================================================================

His Voice is a web and mobile application that presents the complete story of
Jesus Christ — from the first prophecy spoken in the Garden of Eden through every
recorded word He spoke, to the post-resurrection commission. It is not a Bible
app. It is a living, visual, interactive harmony — a single cohesive journey
through the most documented life in human history.


1.1  CORE MISSION STATEMENT
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What This App Does That Nothing Else Does:

  1. Starts before the birth — from Genesis 3:15 through every Messianic
     prophecy across 1,500 years
  2. Shows prophecy fulfilled in real-time as the story progresses
  3. Harmonizes all four Gospels word-by-word — aligning parallel accounts,
     flagging gaps, filling missing context
  4. Displays 3 translations side by side (KJV / WEB / ASV) for every
     passage — ALL public domain, zero licensing cost
  5. Labels convergence score — how many gospels record each event (1–4)
  6. Integrates removed books (Gospel of Thomas, Gospel of Peter, Mary, etc.)
     as parallel witnesses
  7. Anchors every event in secular history (Josephus, Tacitus, Pliny,
     Roman census records)
  8. Animates the entire journey as a cinematic, scroll-driven visual experience


1.2  AUDIENCE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Bible scholars and seminarians seeking a harmony tool
  - Christians who want to read the red letters in full chronological context
  - Skeptics and researchers who want secular confirmation alongside scripture
  - New believers or the curious seeking an accessible entry point
  - Students of history, comparative religion, and ancient literature

The application is organized as a single continuous narrative arc across
seven Acts. Each Act contains multiple Scenes. Each Scene is a structured
data object containing gospel accounts, Jesus's words, prophecy links,
gap analysis, and external witnesses.

4.4.1  ARCHITECTURE — THE THREE BRAINS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  BRAIN 1: THE PATTERN ENGINE (Rule-Based Intelligence)
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Technology: Custom TypeScript engine — zero external dependencies
  Cost: $0 — pure code logic

  What it does:
  - Prophecy-to-fulfillment matching — automatically links OT prophecy text
    to NT events using keyword matching, reference chains, and scored
    relevance
  - Gospel convergence detection — identifies where 2, 3, or 4 gospels
    record the same event and flags differences, additions, omissions
  - Chronological sequencing — resolves timeline conflicts between gospels
    (e.g., John places Temple Clearing early; Synoptics place it late)
  - Thematic threading — groups scenes by theme (healing, parables,
    confrontation, prayer) across all gospels
  - Cross-reference spider — when viewing any verse, automatically surfaces
    every other verse in the database that quotes, alludes to, or parallels it
  - Word frequency analysis — tracks recurring words of Jesus across His
    ministry to show emphasis patterns

  Data structures:
  - keyword_index: Inverted index mapping every significant word → scenes
  - reference_graph: Directed graph of verse → verse cross-references
  - theme_clusters: Pre-computed thematic groupings with confidence scores
  - prophecy_chains: Bidirectional prophecy ↔ fulfillment link chains


  BRAIN 2: THE SEMANTIC ENGINE (AI-Powered Understanding)
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Technology: Transformers.js (Hugging Face) — runs ML models IN THE BROWSER
  Cost: $0 — models downloaded once, cached locally, runs on user's device

  Models used (all free, open-source):
  - all-MiniLM-L6-v2 (~23MB) — Sentence embeddings for semantic search
    Finds passages by MEANING, not just keywords. "Where does Jesus talk
    about forgiveness?" returns relevant passages even if the word
    "forgiveness" doesn't appear.
  - distilbert-base-uncased-finetuned-sst-2 — Sentiment & tone analysis
    Classifies Jesus's words by tone: teaching, warning, compassion,
    authority, prophecy, prayer
  - question-answering model (distilbert-base-cased-distilled-squad) —
    Ask a question, get an answer sourced from the scripture database
    "Who was present at the Transfiguration?" → "Peter, James, and John
    (Matthew 17:1; Mark 9:2; Luke 9:28)"

  How it works:
  1. At first visit, models download (~50MB total) and cache in browser
  2. All 113 scenes + 230 sayings are pre-embedded as vectors
  3. User queries are embedded in real-time and matched via cosine similarity
  4. Results ranked by semantic relevance + convergence score + era context
  5. Zero server calls — everything happens on the user's device

  Vector storage:
  - Pre-computed embeddings stored as Float32Arrays in IndexedDB
  - Supabase pgvector extension used for server-side backup search
  - Both client and server search are FREE


  BRAIN 3: THE INSIGHT ENGINE (Contextual Intelligence — VPS-Powered)
  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Technology: Ollama on VPS (run FULL-SIZE language models server-side) +
              WebLLM (browser fallback) + Custom rule templates
  Cost: $0 software — models are free and open-source, runs on our VPS

  What it does:
  - Scene summaries — generates a 2-3 sentence summary of any scene
    highlighting what makes it unique across the gospels
  - Gap explanations — when a gospel omits an event recorded by others,
    the engine generates a contextual note explaining possible reasons
  - Prophecy probability — calculates and presents the mathematical
    probability of coincidental fulfillment (Peter Stoner method)
  - "Did You Know?" cards — auto-generated insight cards that appear
    alongside each scene, surfacing non-obvious connections
  - Harmony narrator — generates a unified narrative paragraph from
    multiple gospel accounts of the same event
  - Historical context — links scene events to secular history (Josephus,
    Tacitus, etc.) with explanatory context
  - CONVERSATIONAL AI — users can CHAT with the intelligence system about
    any passage, ask follow-up questions, explore connections — powered
    by Ollama running Llama 3 / Mistral on the VPS
  - AUDIO NARRATION — Piper TTS on VPS generates spoken audio for every
    passage in multiple voices and languages

  Models on VPS via Ollama (all free, open-weight):
  ┌────────────────────────────┬────────────┬─────────────────────────────┐

THE AGENT'S GOLDEN RULES — NEVER VIOLATED
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  RULE 1: NEVER USE THE BIBLE TO CONVINCE SOMEONE WHO REJECTS THE BIBLE
  If the user shows skepticism toward scripture, the agent MUST start
  with secular evidence, scientific studies, mathematical probability,
  hostile witnesses, and cross-tradition confirmation. The Bible becomes
  a valid source only AFTER its historical credibility has been
  established through external evidence.

  RULE 2: ALWAYS START WHERE THE QUESTIONER STANDS
  Detect the user's current level from their question. "Why should I
  believe in an afterlife?" = Level 0. "Was Jesus real?" = Level 1.
  "Did He really rise?" = Level 2. Never jump levels.

  RULE 3: HOSTILE WITNESSES FIRST, FRIENDLY WITNESSES SECOND
  A Roman governor confirming Jesus's execution is more powerful to a
  skeptic than a gospel account. The Talmud confirming Jesus's miracles
  (calling them "sorcery") is more powerful than Matthew saying He healed
  people. The Quran confirming the virgin birth is more powerful than
  Luke saying it. ALWAYS lead with the sources your questioner would
  least expect to support the claim.

  RULE 4: NEVER BE PREACHY — LET THE EVIDENCE SPEAK
  The agent never says "you should believe" or "the truth is." It says
  "here's what the evidence shows" and "here's what 10 separate
  civilizations independently recorded." The evidence is so overwhelming
  that it speaks for itself. The agent's job is to PRESENT, not PERSUADE.

  RULE 5: ALWAYS ACKNOWLEDGE THE STRONGEST COUNTER-ARGUMENT
  Before presenting evidence, the agent acknowledges the strongest
  objection the skeptic might raise — and addresses it head-on.
  "You might say these are just ancient myths. Here's why that
  explanation doesn't hold up..." This builds trust. The agent never
  hides from hard questions.

  RULE 6: THE "NO PROPHECY FROM A MAN" PRINCIPLE
  No human being who existed before Christ could have written prophecies
  that are 100% accurate about events 400–1,500 years in the future.
  Even if someone claims the Bible was fabricated, they cannot explain:
  a. The Dead Sea Scrolls (physically dated, independently verified)
  b. 24 prophecies from different authors across 1,500 years
  c. All fulfilled by one person who had no control over most of them
     (birthplace, lineage, manner of execution, burial location)
  d. Mathematical probability of coincidence: beyond calculable
  This is the agent's STRONGEST card and it uses it strategically —
  not too early (or the skeptic isn't ready) and not too late.

  RULE 7: ADDRESS THE "CONTROL" ARGUMENT DIRECTLY
  When someone says "religion was invented to control people":
  a. Christianity was founded by executed prisoners and persecuted
     minorities — the LEAST powerful people in the Roman Empire
  b. The Bible commands giving away wealth, serving the poor, loving
     enemies, and forgiving oppressors — the worst possible propaganda
     for maintaining power
  c. The first Christians GAINED nothing — they lost everything.
     Homes, families, freedom, lives. No one invents a control mechanism
     that costs them everything and gains them torture.
  d. The religions that DID function as control mechanisms (state
     religions, divine-right monarchies) look nothing like early
     Christianity

  RULE 8: THE EVIDENCE CHAIN MUST BE UNBREAKABLE
  Each piece of evidence the agent presents must logically lead to the
  next. The chain must be so tight that rejecting one piece requires
  the skeptic to also reject principles they already accept (science,
  mathematics, historical methodology, eyewitness testimony standards).

  Example chain:
  1. "You accept scientific studies?" → Yes
  2. "The AWARE Study shows consciousness during brain death" → Evidence
  3. "You accept mathematics?" → Yes
  4. "The odds of 24 prophecies fulfilled by chance are 1 in 10^157" → Math
  5. "You accept hostile witnesses?" → Yes
  6. "The Talmud, Tacitus, and the Quran all confirm key facts" → Evidence
  7. "You accept that people don't die for known lies?" → Yes
  8. "All 12 disciples chose death over recanting" → Historical fact
  9. Now the question isn't faith vs. evidence. The question is: what
     CONCLUSION does all this evidence point to?

    Query: { page_context }

  SECTION 9 — CONTENT PHILOSOPHY & EDITORIAL STANDARDS
================================================================================

9.1  SCHOLARLY INTEGRITY
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - All Gospel Harmony cross-references must cite established harmonies
    (e.g., A.T. Robertson's Harmony of the Gospels)
  - Gap analysis notes must distinguish between:
      (a) Different perspectives on the same event
      (b) Possibly different events recorded similarly
      (c) Theological emphasis differences between writers
  - External sources must be labeled: canonical / deuterocanonical /
    apocryphal / secular historical
  - No denominational bias in gap notes — present evidence, let reader conclude
  - Dates labeled as 'c.' (circa) where precise dating is uncertain
  - Prophecy fulfillments labeled by type:
      Direct Fulfillment / Typological / Allusion / Verbal Echo


9.2  TRANSLATION INTEGRITY — ALL PUBLIC DOMAIN
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  ALL THREE TRANSLATIONS ARE FREE AND PUBLIC DOMAIN — ZERO LICENSING:

  - KJV (King James Version, 1611): Public domain (pre-1924). The most
    recognized English translation. No licensing required.
  - WEB (World English Bible, 2000): Public domain. Modern English, no
    copyright holder. No licensing required.
  - ASV (American Standard Version, 1901): Public domain. Highly literal
    word-for-word translation. Preferred by scholars for accuracy. No
    licensing required.

  NOTE: The Amplified Bible was removed from the plan because it requires
  a commercial license from Zondervan. The ASV provides equivalent literal
  accuracy at zero cost, keeping the entire app 100% free.

  - All references: Standard notation — Book Chapter:Verse (e.g., John 3:16)
  - Jesus's words: Gold styling consistently across all translations


9.3  WORLD SOURCES EDITORIAL STANDARD
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every source page must open with a clear editorial statement covering:
  - The religious or historical tradition it belongs to
  - Historical date of composition and authorship
  - Scholarly consensus on its reliability and historical value
  - Which canonical gospel events it parallels, confirms, or contradicts
  - What unique perspective or information it contributes
  - Why it was excluded from the canonical Bible (if applicable)
  - Which traditions still consider it authoritative (if applicable)

STANDARDS BY CATEGORY:
  - Christian apocrypha: Explain why excluded from canon, what value remains
  - Islamic sources: Present Quranic text with Arabic + English, note
    parallels and differences respectfully, cite Islamic scholarly context
  - Jewish sources: Present in historical context; note hostile vs neutral
    tone where applicable; acknowledge Josephus interpolation debates
  - Roman/Greek: Note that these are non-Christian confirmations of key
    events — hostile witnesses are especially valuable historically
  - Eastern/Other: Present as comparative context; note scholarly consensus
    on whether references are genuine or later interpolations

The goal is not to argue for or against any tradition — it is to present
the fullest possible historical picture of how Jesus is attested across
ALL of human civilization. We are not limited by any single tradition.

   belongs in this record."
